US Census Bureau (2018)

For the Census Bureau, we've determine address frame for 2020 Census, decennial census test, and American Community Survey. Our main priority comes from applying address filters and analyzing census test for address frame development to produce research estimates on more than 20 GB of data. Sampling methods and statistical modeling are used. For more information, please fill out the contact form.

Gates Millennium Scholars Project (2019)

The Gates Millennium Scholars (GMS) Project consists of 15 years of scholarship data to research impact of demographics as well as other factors on scholar migration and further graduate studies using statistical methods and modeling. For more information, please fill out the contact form.

CADE

As an analyst in CADE, I've used data manipulation, data mining methods, image processing/neural network (OCR), and natural language processing to organize data in the CADE system. For more information, please refer to the CADE website.
